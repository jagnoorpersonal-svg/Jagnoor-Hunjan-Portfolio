Context Analysis Report
IOM UN Migration × UK International Development
Women in Southwest and Galmudug, Somalia play key roles in peacebuilding and community safety but remain excluded from formal systems. Structural barriers limit their political participation and access to justice, especially in GBV cases. The report calls for inclusive governance, survivor-centered justice, and stronger support for women-led initiatives.
